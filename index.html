Visual Computing @ TCS Research

We at visual computing research group works on research problems related to synthesis, estimation, animation, and rendering of objects and scenes across space and time. Currently, we are focusing on building algorithms for,

  • automation in creative content creation using AI
  • interactive digital representation of physical environment
  • speech and emotion aware body gesture and facial expressions for 2D and 3D digital humans
  • re-rendered reality for Metaverse

To this end, we are solving various research problems such as, creating a 3D model of the surrounding world, building a personalized digital 3D avatar of humans including cloth and hair animation, 2D/3D conversational agents with expresive gestures and expressions, making 3D human avatars to interact with other objects in the 3D digital scene, and real-time rendering of the large sdynamic scene with the varying spatial lighting and reflectance.
